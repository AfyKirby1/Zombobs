Survive endless waves of zombies in this fast-paced top-down arena shooter. Enhanced with critical hits, hit markers, spawn indicators, off-screen threat warnings, reload progress bars, floating damage numbers, bullet trails, shell casings, screen shake, directional blood splatter, menu music, and horror atmosphere effects.
